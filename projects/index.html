MaPU


A Mathematical Computing Architecture. </br> MaPU was based on my PhD dissertation which assembles the data path based on program flow. From 2009 - 2016 when I worked as a research scientist at Chinese Academy of Science, I lead the team (initially only PhD students) to implement the architecture spanning from programming model, software and hardware.

Celerity


Open-Source RISC-V Tiered Accelerator Fabric SoC.</br> I was one of the most contributors on Celerity projects when I worked in Michael Taylor's group at University of California, San Diego, from 2016 -- 2017. I was in charge of the manycore array, SoC assembling and software stack.

SDH


Software Defined Hardware.</br> I was fortunate to participate the SDH program when I was a research scientist in Michael Taylor's group at University of Washington (2017 -- 2019), leading the hardware and low-level runtime group.
